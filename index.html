Circle of Fifths is a scheme that has been known since the beginning of the written history. It shows the harmonic relations on a circle in a symmetrical way.  Tritonet is a visual model that introduces extra components on Circle of Fifths, which allows it to work as a musical slide-rule. Table: Right tightens, Left resolves. Top is the Center and it can only hold big letters. White notes are one accidental higher, black ones are one accidental lower. Pick black from left, white from right.   Night: Alters the accidentals of the notes and reduces the population of notes to seven, in order to build Natural Modes. If a note from left is at night, sharpens. If a note from right is at night, flattens.  Compass: Allows to pick notes in order to write counterpoint or build chords. Tip of the compass shows the lowest notes of the chord. Main body holds the harmonic consonances, edge of the wings modal neighbours and the wings melodic intervals.    Weight of the notes also reveals the tendency for the next move. This version only demonstrates the strong root triads, white from the right wing and black from the bottom, which has a tendency towards left.   Design and Code by : Tolga Zafer Özdemir  www.tolgazaferozdemir.com
